Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to questions you may have. Can't find what you're looking for? come Contact Us!

Are your products authentic? ▼
Yes, we work directly with local artisans, cooperatives, and small businesses across the Philippines. This ensures that every item is made by skilled craftsmen who are deeply rooted in their cultural traditions. We provide details about the artisans, their techniques, and the cultural significance of the item. This transparency allows you to connect with the product on a deeper level. By purchasing from Dangal, you’re supporting local livelihoods and helping to preserve traditional crafts. A portion of our proceeds goes back to the communities, ensuring that these practices continue for generations.
Where do you ship to?▼
We are pleased to offer shipping services to various locations across the Philippines! We currently deliver to major cities and provinces, including Metro Manila, Cebu, Davao, Quezon City, Makati, Taguig, and many more. To see if we ship to your specific area, simply enter your address during checkout, and we’ll confirm if we can deliver to you.
How much is shipping?▼
Shipping costs depend on the destination and the weight of your order. Here’s a general guideline for our shipping rates within the Philippines:

Metro Manila:

Standard Shipping: ₱50 - ₱100

Express Shipping: ₱100 - ₱200



Luzon (Outside Metro Manila):

Standard Shipping: ₱100 - ₱180

Express Shipping: ₱180 - ₱300



Visayas:

Standard Shipping: ₱150 - ₱250

Express Shipping: ₱250 - ₱400



Mindanao:

Standard Shipping: ₱180 - ₱250

Express Shipping: ₱300 - ₱500



Please note that shipping costs may vary based on promotional offers, special items, or specific courier fees. For orders above a certain amount, we may offer free shipping promotions, so be sure to check our latest offers.

How long will it take to receive my order?▼
Delivery times depend on your location and the shipping method you choose. Here’s a general estimate for shipping durations within the Philippines:

Metro Manila:

Standard Shipping: 1 - 3 business days

Express Shipping: 1 - 2 business days



Luzon (Outside Metro Manila):

Standard Shipping: 3 - 5 business days

Express Shipping: 2 - 4 business days



Visayas:

Standard Shipping: 4 - 7 business day

Express Shipping: 3 - 5 business days



Mindanao:

Standard Shipping: 5 - 8 business days

Express Shipping: 4 - 6 business days



Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary slightly due to factors such as weather conditions, holidays, or courier service delays. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email so you can monitor its progress.

What is your return policy?▼
We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please ensure the product is unused and in original packaging.
When will I receive my refund?▼
Refunds are processed within 7 days after we receive your returned item.
Can I sell my products on your website?▼
Yes, you can sell your native and/or post-modern Filipino-made products on the website as long as you are a Filipino seller or store located from any region in the Philippines.
How can I sell and advertise my products on your website?▼
You can sell and advertise your products by paying P500 (per month) to affiliate with us. Number of months per seller/store will be limited up to 24 months. For more details, please contact our email at DangalCorp@gmail.com. If you want to avail, fill out the google form provided.
How will I benefit as a seller for advertising my products on your website?▼
The following are the benefits that you will get from availing the “membership”:

1. Promoting and advertising your products on the website

2. Regular notice regard forthcoming bazaars, exhibits, the sellers/business owners can join

3. Coordination with schools for field trip events and potential on-the-job pool

4. Link up to hotels, restaurants, etc for increased visibility and revenue generation.
